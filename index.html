Services

Witeroo Inc's people-first approach is based on collaboration, trust and respect in our services, ensuring we reach client's profitability and productivity goals, while continuously evolving, and using well-known technologies to deliver on promises.

Project Management

We use agile project management and PMI best practices, tools and frameworks to manage medium to large-scale projects, in engineering and technology. Consistency and transparency in the PM process result in on-time delivery of products and deliverables.

Industrial Automation

Based on experiences in control systems, automation engineering and industrial robotics. We will provide distinct capabilities/services in control software design, integration, factory acceptance testing (UAT/SAT), on-site supervision, commissioning, performance verification and production support.

Cloud Solutions

Cloud solutions like compute, storage, networking, database and other on-demand resources on cloud provider platforms. We use technology tools for infrastructure automation, application deployment, architecture design, resource provisioning and orchestration and monitoring of containerized applications.
