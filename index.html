Services

Witeroo Inc's people-first approach is based on collaboration, trust and respect in our dealings and the services we render. This ensures Witeroo Inc's ability to reach client profitability and productivity goals. We continuously evolve, using well-known technologies to meet objectives and deliver on promises.

Project Management

From waterfall to agile methodologies, Witeroo Inc. uses PMI standard best practices, agile tools and frameworks including kanban and scrum to manage medium to large-scale projects, in engineering, software development and technology. These ensure consistency and transparency in the PM process, leading to on-time delivery of products, deliverables and objectives to stakeholders and clients.

Industrial Automation

Based on experience in industrial automation engineering, from control systems to automation engineering and industrial robotics. Witeroo Inc can provide distinct capabilities/services in control software designing, automation integration, project management, factory acceptance testing (UAT/SAT), on-site supervision and commissioning, and performance verification to customer clients.

Cloud Solutions

Provision of cloud-based solutions like compute, storage, networking, database and other on-demand resources accessible through the internet on cloud provider platforms. We also use well-known cloud tools for infrastructure automation, application deployment, architecture design, resource provisioning as well as orchestration and monitoring of containerized applications.
